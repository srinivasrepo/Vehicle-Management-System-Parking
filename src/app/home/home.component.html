Parking Management System constitutes

Realtime Vehicle counting –The vehicle counting system constitutes of a non intrusive vision based vehicle counting system. This will be installed in every level such that the management gets real time knowledge of the number of cars present in every level. Real time parking guidance display –Display boards displays the number of empty slots at every level. Video Surveillance (optional) –An optionalmulti camera video surveillance system can be integrated into solution.
